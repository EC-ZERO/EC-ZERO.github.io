Research Directions

Bridging atomic-scale science with industrial-scale energy devices through disruptive materials and device engineering.

Spin-promoted Electrocatalysis

Spin regulation offers a new paradigm to overcome fundamental activity and selectivity limitations in electrochemical reactions. We investigate how magnetic structures and spin-polarized electronic states govern reaction intermediates and catalytic pathways.

Hydrogen Production Technology

Hydrogen is widely regarded as the ultimate energy carrier for enabling a zero-carbon energy system. We develop integrated electrochemical strategies for diversified hydrogen production from water, ammonia, alcohols, and biomass-derived feedstocks.

Membrane Electrolyzer Innovation

Advanced membrane electrolyzers are essential for translating catalytic discoveries into scalable technologies. We develop solid and semi-solid electrolysis systems by engineering membrane electrodes and device architectures for electrochemical conversion.
