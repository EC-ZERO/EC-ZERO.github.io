Research Directions

Shaping future electrocatalysis through catalytic frontier, materials innovation, and transformative device.

Spin-promoted Electrocatalysis

Spin regulation offers a new paradigm to overcome fundamental activity and selectivity limitations in electrochemical reactions. We enhance the intrinsic activity of electrocatalyst by investigating spin polarization mechanism during catalysis.

Hydrogen Production Technology

Hydrogen is widely regarded as a cornerstone energy carrier for a zero-carbon future. Our research focuses on versatile electrochemical pathways for efficient hydrogen generation from water, ammonia, alcohols, and renewable biomass resources.

Smart Electrolysis Innovation

Driven by the advance of AI and Intelligent grid, our membrane electrolysis system is becoming "smart" in integrating green hydrogen economy with seawater desalination, biomass valorization, and waste upcycling, advancing the sustainability.
