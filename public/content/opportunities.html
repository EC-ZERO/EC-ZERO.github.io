The EC-ZERO Lab is dedicated to pioneering electrocatalytic innovations from fundamental science to transformative technologies. We are building a dynamic and interdisciplinary research team to tackle grand challenges. We are currently recruiting graduate students and research fellows, including:

PhD Students (2–3 positions)

  • Strong background in electrochemistry
  • Experience with nanomaterials synthesis
  • Good communication skills in English

Postdoctoral Researchers (2–3 positions)

  • PhD in relevant field
  • Proven track record of publications
  • Expertise in operando spectroscopy preferred

Master students (2–3 positions)

  • PhD in relevant field
  • Proven track record of publications
  • Expertise in operando spectroscopy preferred

Undergraduate students

  • can join with University- or School-level programmes (e.g. Puyuan Future Scholar Plan).
  • Proven track record of publications
  • Expertise in operando spectroscopy preferred

Successful candidates will work on frontier topics including spin-promoted electrocatalysis, diversified hydrogen production, and next-generation membrane electrolyzer technologies. Our group offers an international research environment, access to state-of-the-art facilities, and strong opportunities for academic development and industrial collaboration. We welcome applicants with backgrounds in electrochemistry, materials science, chemical engineering, physics, energy engineering, DFT/microkinetic modelling or related disciplines. Highly motivated individuals with creativity, teamwork spirit, and strong scientific curiosity are especially encouraged to apply. To apply, please send to tianze.wu##sjtu.edu.cn your CV, research interests, and representative publications (if available).
