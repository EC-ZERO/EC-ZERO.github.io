Research Directions

We bridge the gap between fundamental surface science and practical energy devices through innovative materials design.

Single-Atom Catalysts

Developing atomically dispersed metal catalysts with 100% atom utilization for highly efficient OER and ORR processes.

CO2 Reduction (CO2RR)

Converting carbon dioxide into valuable fuels and chemicals using renewable electricity and tailored catalyst interfaces.

Next-Gen Fuel Cells

Engineering membrane electrode assemblies (MEAs) for high-performance proton exchange membrane fuel cells (PEMFCs).
